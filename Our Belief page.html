Our Belief

"Teaching a child not to step on a caterpillar is as valuable to the child as it is to the caterpillar."
~Bradley Miller
We believe in the Golden Rule "Do not do unto others what you would not want them to do unto you" or in other words, "Always treat others as you wish to be treated yourself."
Finally we firmly believe that
"The greatness of a nation and its moral progress can be judged by the ways its animals are treated."
~Mahatma Gandhi
