Our Mission

Animal Rescuer's mission is to sow the seeds of empathy for people, compassion for animals and care for the environment in the hearts of children, so as to prevent cruelty to all living beings and also to build awareness and implement effective systems such that habitats and animals are protected.We accomplish our mission by pursuing a two-pronged approach: Providing information about First Aid and Providing a very active animal helpline.
